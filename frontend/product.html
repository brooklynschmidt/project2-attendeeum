The last calendar app you'll ever need

Attendeum

Attendeum is a web-application that allows users to post events that they plan on attending or hosting to a calendar that their friends can view and add to. Share events, track RSVPs, and never miss out on plans with friends again.

Demo

Add Events

Easily add events to your calendar with screenshots or manual entry. Include descriptions, dates, locations, and invite your friends all in one place.

Demo

Share your Calendar

Let your friends see what events you're attending. Share your calendar with specific people or make it visible to your entire friend group.

Demo

Seamless RSVP

RSVP to events with a single click. See who else is going and get notified when friends join events you're interested in.

G-Cal Sync

Sync your Attendeum calendar with Google Calendar. Keep all your events in one place and never double-book yourself again.
